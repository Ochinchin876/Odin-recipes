Lasagna

Its a delicious lasagna

Lasagna, famed worldwide, has ancient origins and remains an Italian superstar dish. Basically, lasagna is a baked casserole made with wide flat pasta and layered with fillings such as ragú, bechamel, vegetables and various cheeses. Unsurprisingly, every Italian region has its own traditional recipe.

Ingredients

  • dried sheets of pasta
  • rich meat ragú
  • ricotta
  • mozzarella

Steps

  1. After the initial sauce layer, add a layer of pasta sheets, ricotta mixture (or bechamel), sauce, and cheese
  2. repeat the layers
  3. Top the last layer of your lasagna with sauce and cheese
  4. You can also alternate layers of sauce and ricotta cheese
